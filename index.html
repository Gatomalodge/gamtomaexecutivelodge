Lodging

Our exclusive executive lodge offers comfortable and spacious rooms with modern amenities. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, and a private bathroom. We have a variety of room types to choose from, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and suites.

Food Services

In addition to our restaurant, we also offer room service and catering for events. Our room service menu features a variety of dishes that can be delivered directly to your room. Our catering services are perfect for weddings, conferences, and other special events.

Conference Room

Our exclusive executive lodge has a spacious and fully-equipped conference room. The room is perfect for business meetings, seminars, and other events. We provide all the necessary equipment, including projectors, screens, and sound systems.
